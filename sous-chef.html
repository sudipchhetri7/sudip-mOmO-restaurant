Sous Chef Vacancy

We are looking for an experienced Sous Chef to join our fine dining team at mOmO Restaurant. The ideal candidate should have a passion for gourmet cuisine and exceptional attention to detail.

  • Assist the Head Chef in daily operations.
  • Supervise kitchen staff and ensure quality.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of experience as a Sous Chef.
  • Culinary degree or equivalent experience.
  • Excellent leadership and organizational skills.

If you are passionate about fine dining and culinary excellence, we would love to hear from you!
