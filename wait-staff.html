Wait Staff Vacancy

We are looking for passionate and experienced wait staff to provide exceptional service in our fine dining restaurant.

Job Responsibilities

  • Take food and beverage orders accurately.
  • Provide excellent customer service and ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Set up and maintain the cleanliness of tables and service areas.

Qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience as a wait staff in a fine dining environment.
  • Great communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

If you enjoy working in hospitality and providing top-notch customer service, we would love to have you join our team!
